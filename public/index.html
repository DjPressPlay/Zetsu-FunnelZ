What is a Funnel?

A funnel is a strategic marketing system that guides visitors through a journey—from initial awareness to becoming customers. Like water flowing through a funnel, potential customers move through stages, with each step designed to build trust, engagement, and conversion.

Classic Marketing Funnel

The traditional 4-stage funnel: Awareness → Interest → Decision → Action. Guide prospects through each stage to maximize conversions.

Sales Conversion Funnel

Track your sales pipeline from initial contact to closed deal. Optimize each stage to increase your conversion rate.
